Organizations

Below you will find some great organizations that I try to support. Please give them a look!

Women Who Code is a great organization whose mission is to inspire women to excel in technology careers. You can find out more about them here!

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers is an organization that is very near and dear to my heart. I was a member and eventually president of my university's chapter for all four years. I had many great experiences, and was afforded many great opportunities through them. SHPE's mission is to changes lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support, and development. You can find out more about them here!
